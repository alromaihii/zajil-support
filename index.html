📚 About

Zajil-News is a real-time news monitoring app built for the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) region. It uses AI to score every event by severity, tracks live financial markets, delivers push notifications for critical developments, and provides AI-generated situation briefs.

Designed for analysts, journalists, investors, and anyone who needs to stay informed about the Gulf region.

⚡ Features

📰 News Dashboard

AI-scored events with interactive map, filters by region and severity, auto-scrolling carousel.

📈 Finance Dashboard

Live indices, stocks, commodities, forex. Market charts, watchlist, and curated finance news.

🧠 AI Insights

AI-generated situation briefs, per-country threat assessments, key trends, and watch items.

🔔 Alerts

Push notifications for critical events. Severity-ranked alerts with source attribution.

📺 Live TV

Five live news channels: Al Arabiya, AJ English, AJ Arabic, Sky News, France 24.

🖼 Widgets

Home screen widgets: top alert, threat level, alerts list, full dashboard. Tap to open articles.

🌎 Bilingual

Full English and Arabic support with right-to-left layout.

🗺 Interactive Map

Color-coded pins by severity. Tap to read. Standard and satellite views.

🎯 Severity Scoring

Every event is scored 1-10 by AI based on its potential impact on the GCC region.

8-10 Critical
5-7 Elevated
1-4 Low

❓ FAQ

What devices are supported?

iPhone and iPad running iOS 18.6 or later.

How are events scored?

AI analyzes each event's potential impact on the GCC region and assigns a 1-10 severity score. Red = critical, Orange = elevated, Blue = low.

Is the app free?

Yes, completely free with no in-app purchases.

How do I report a bug?

Use the TestFlight feedback feature (shake your device) or email us below.

How often does data refresh?

News events refresh every 5 minutes. Market data updates every 2 minutes.
