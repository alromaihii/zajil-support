About

Zajil is a real-time news monitoring app focused on the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) region. It uses AI to score events by severity, tracks financial markets, and delivers push notifications for critical developments.

Features

  • AI-scored news events (1-10 severity scale)
  • Interactive map with event pins
  • Live market data: indices, stocks, commodities, forex
  • AI-generated situation briefs and threat assessments
  • Push notifications for critical events
  • Home screen widgets
  • Bilingual support (English and Arabic)
  • Live TV news channels

FAQ

What devices are supported?

Zajil runs on iPhone and iPad with iOS 18.6 or later.

How are news events scored?

Each event is scored 1-10 by AI based on its potential impact on the GCC region. Red (8-10) = critical, Orange (5-7) = elevated, Blue (1-4) = low.

Is the app free?

Yes, Zajil is free to use.
